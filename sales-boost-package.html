Sales Conversion Phase — Deliverables & Why They Matter

Launch Ad Campaigns (Paid Ads on Social & Search)

Set up and run targeted ad campaigns — using Facebook/Instagram, Google (or relevant platforms) — with custom creatives and messaging to reach people most likely to buy. This gives your store the reach and visibility needed to turn interest into actual sales.

Competitor & Market Analysis to Refine Positioning & Offers

Analyze competitors, pricing, and market trends to sharpen your product positioning, pricing strategy, and messaging. This helps ensure your offers and campaigns stand out — not just more ads, but smarter ads. .

Optimized Ad Creatives + Landing Pages (Copy + Visuals + Funnel Alignment)

Create ad visuals + copy that match landing page content for consistency and clarity — essential so when people click ads, they arrive at pages that convert. This reduces friction and increases the likelihood of purchase.

Cart‑/Browse‑Abandonment Retargeting & Reminder Flows (Email/SMS/Ads Retargeting)

For visitors who add to cart or browse but don’t buy — set up retargeting (ads, emails, or SMS) to remind them and nudge them back. This helps recover lost sales and increases conversion efficiency.

Social Proof & User‑Generated Content (UGC), Reviews & Testimonials Integration

Encourage and integrate reviews, customer testimonials, or user‑generated content — showing real people using and liking products increases trust, reduces hesitation, and boosts sales conversions.

Email Marketing & Remarketing Campaigns (For Leads & Existing Customers)

Use email flows to reach subscribers and past visitors — offers, promotions, reminders — to convert leads into buyers, and one-time buyers into repeat customers. Effective for conversions and maximizing lifetime value.

Performance Tracking & Data‑Driven Optimization (Analytics, A/B Testing, Conversion Metrics Tracking)

Set up tracking for key metrics (traffic source, conversion rate, ROAS, cart abandonment, etc.), and continuously analyze + optimize campaigns and funnels — so you know what works and scale accordingly.

Retargeting + Lookalike / Segmented Audience Targeting

Build retargeting audiences (past visitors, cart‑abandoners) and lookalike audiences (people similar to best customers) for more efficient ad spend and higher‑probability conversions.
